💪 Why your body needs exercise?

Your body needs regular exercise because it is essentially the ultimate maintenance routine for human biology We evolved to move, and when we don't, our physical and mental systems start to sluggishly decline.

☀ Quick Health Tips

  • 🍎 Eat 5 fruits and veggies every day
  • 💧 Drink 8 glasses of water daily
  • 😴 Sleep 9 to 11 hours every night
  • 🏃 Move your body for 60 minutes
